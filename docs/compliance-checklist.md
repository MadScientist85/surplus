# HBU Asset Recovery Compliance Checklist

## TCPA 2025 Compliance

### SMS Communications
- [ ] Obtain explicit written consent before sending SMS
- [ ] Include clear opt-out mechanism (e.g., "Reply STOP to opt-out")
- [ ] Respect quiet hours (no SMS between 9 PM - 8 AM local time)
- [ ] Honor opt-out requests within 24 hours
- [ ] Maintain frequency cap (max 3 SMS per week per lead)
- [ ] Include sender identification in every message
- [ ] Keep records of all consent and opt-outs for 4 years

### Email Communications
- [ ] Obtain consent before sending marketing emails
- [ ] Include clear unsubscribe link in every email
- [ ] Honor unsubscribe requests within 10 business days
- [ ] Include physical mailing address (CAN-SPAM requirement)
- [ ] Use non-deceptive subject lines
- [ ] Identify message as advertisement when applicable

### Phone Calls
- [ ] Check against National DNC Registry before calling
- [ ] Respect state-specific DNC lists
- [ ] No calls before 8 AM or after 9 PM local time
- [ ] Identify caller and purpose within first 15 seconds
- [ ] Maintain internal DNC list
- [ ] Record keeping for all DNC scrubs (3 years minimum)

## Ethics Shield Requirements

### Lead Screening
- [ ] Screen for recent death/grief indicators
- [ ] Check against known litigator database
- [ ] Verify claim amount reasonability
- [ ] AI ethics analysis for vulnerable populations
- [ ] Block leads that fail ethics threshold (0.8)

### Prohibited Practices
- [ ] Never contact leads in active grief situations
- [ ] No high-pressure tactics or urgency language
- [ ] No guaranteed recovery promises
- [ ] No contact with known serial litigators
- [ ] No pursuit of claims under $1,000 without clear benefit to claimant

## Data Privacy & Security

### GDPR/CCPA Compliance
- [ ] Provide clear privacy policy
- [ ] Enable data access requests
- [ ] Enable data deletion requests
- [ ] Maintain data processing records
- [ ] Report breaches within 72 hours

### Data Retention
- [ ] Audit logs: 365 days minimum
- [ ] Communication records: 4 years
- [ ] Consent records: 4 years
- [ ] Financial records: 7 years

## Emergency Procedures

### Compliance Violation Detected
1. Immediately cease violating activity
2. Log violation in audit system
3. Alert compliance officer
4. Document remediation steps
5. Report to relevant authorities if required

### Data Breach
1. Activate incident response team
2. Contain breach
3. Assess scope of breach
4. Notify affected parties within 72 hours
5. Report to authorities as required
6. Document incident and response
